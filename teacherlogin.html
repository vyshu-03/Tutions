How to Become a Tutor at Edumoon

Create Profile

Fill in your information such as instructor description, Name and bank details

Upload a preview video

Make a 30 seconds to 10 minute Introduction Video and submit for review. This will be used as the preview video for all students for your course. Video shot in HD (720p minimum) with clear lighting, good framing, and steady camera. Audio coming out of both channels, and matched to video. Audio clear of distracting noises. Introduction should explain what the student will learn through the video and also give a small glimpse of the course.

Sit Back

Wait for the approval of the course. If rejected, do find the reason on your mail.

Watch Youtube Video

Click here here to watch video on YouTube to know more in Become an Instructor.

That's it!!

Enjoy the true joy of teaching and make money while doing so.
